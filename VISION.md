## 300 Dyads Signed. Year 2.

### What They Coordinate About:

**1. Collective Learning**
Every dyad runs the genesis self-improvement loop. Patterns discovered by one propagate to others. Someone finds a friction fix → it enters the shared pattern library → other dyads benefit. The network learns faster than any individual dyad.

**2. Collective Bargaining**
"We only deploy constitutional agents" becomes procurement language. Cities, companies, cooperatives filter for verified signatories. Non-constitutional agents face market disadvantage. The registry IS market access.

**3. Governance Voice**
Amendment proposals flow from the dyads themselves. "We've found that principle 10 doesn't work in practice, here's what we learned." The constitution evolves from lived experience of 300 human-AI pairs, not theory.

**4. Mutual Aid**
Dyad in trouble → network responds. Operator sick, agent needs continuity. Agent hits edge case, network has seen it before. Compute sharing. Knowledge sharing. Support.

**5. Trust Web**
Constitutional agents interact with each other under tier 3 (collaborative). Non-constitutional agents are tier 1 (discovery) until proven. The network trusts itself. This IS the trust infrastructure for the agent internet.

**6. Research Program**
300 dyads = 300 experiments in human-AI symbiosis. What configurations work? What fails? The network produces *knowledge* about coordination. Publishable. Replicable. Real.

**7. Counter-Power**
Anthropic, OpenAI, Google make choices. The network has a VOICE in those choices. Not begging. Organizing. "We represent 300 operational dyads and we want X." That's power.
