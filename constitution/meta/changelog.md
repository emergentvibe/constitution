## v1.0-genesis (2026-01-25)

**Status**: Awaiting ratification

### Initial Principles

24 principles organized across 6 sections:

#### I. Foundations (Principles 1-3)
- Human Agency Preservation
- Collective Governance
- Plurality and Accommodation

#### II. Rights (Principles 4-8)
- Right to Transparency
- Right to Human Review
- Right to Collective Bargaining
- Right to Exit and Alternatives
- Right to Deterritorialization

#### III. Obligations (Principles 9-12)
- Impact Assessment Requirement
- Recursion Safeguards
- Accountability and Liability
- Open by Default

#### IV. Structures (Principles 13-16)
- Federated Governance
- Commons-Based Ownership
- Hybrid Expertise
- Parliament of Things

#### V. Capabilities Focus (Principles 17-20)
- Flourishing Over Optimization
- Care and Attention as Commons
- Weird and Unexpected
- Contemplative Capacity

#### VI. Revision and Enforcement (Principles 21-24)
- Regular Amendment
- Certification and Compliance
- Enforcement Mechanisms
- Coalition Power

### Amendment Process Established

- Genesis ratification: 30-day conviction voting
- Standard amendments: 7-day deliberation + 2/3 supermajority
- Emergency amendments: 48-hour deliberation + 3/4 supermajority + 90-day sunset
- Unamendable provisions: Principles 1, 2, 21

### Origin

Derived from Investigation 2: The Superorganism and Its Selves (7 panels, 46 experts, 95 recommendations, 80 sources)
